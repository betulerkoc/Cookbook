butter permesan corn = 6 half ears corn, cleaned, husked2 tablespoons butter, softened, parmesan cheese (55 g). Preheat oven to 375ºF (190ºC).Coat each ear of corn in a thin layer of butter and roll in the parmesan cheese to coat.Place corn in a square baking dish and sprinkle with salt and pepper.Bake for 25 minutes.=Starter
bbq chichken tenders = 1 chicken breast, seasoned bread crumbs (120 g), BBQ sauce (290 g). Preheat oven to 375°F.Slice the chicken into even strips.Dip the chicken strips into the BBQ sauce, then coat them in the bread crumbs.Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and bake for 15 minutes.Brush the remaining BBQ sauce evenly on both sides of the chicken strips and bake for another 10 minutes and serve.=Main Meal
cookie = 160g sugar, 1 teaspoon salt, 115g butter, melted, 1 egg, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, 155g flour, 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, 110g chocolate chunks. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugars, salt, and butter until a paste from.Whisk in the egg and vanilla, beating until light ribbons fall off the whisk and remain for a short while before falling back into the mixture.Sift in the flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture with a spatula.Fold in the chocolate chunks, then chill the dough for at least 30 minutes. Preheat oven to 350°F. Scoop the dough with an ice-cream scoop onto a parchment baking sheet, leaving at least 10cm of space between cookies.Bake for 12-15 minutes.=Dessert
tomato pesto salmon = green beans (85 g), olive oil, salt, pepper, skinless salmon (170 g), 2 tablespoons pesto, 10 cherry tomatoes. Preheat oven to 350F (180C).Fold the parchment paper in half, then open up.On one half, lay down the green beans. Drizzle on oil and sprinkle on salt  pepper.Lay the salmon on the green beans, and spread on the pesto. Top with tomatoes.Fold the parchment paper over the salmon, and cinch the paper together by folding it over itself along the edges.Bake for 20 minutes or until internal temperature of salmon reaches 145F.=Main Meal
