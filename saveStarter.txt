sweet and sour eggplant = 2 medium eggplant, oil (120 mL), 1 tablespoon cumin seed, 2 tablespoons garlic, onion (225 g), chopped, 2 tablespoons red pepper flakes, ketchup (240 g), vinegar (237 mL), 1 tablespoon salt, water, as needed.Cut the eggplant into medium sized pieces.In a frying pan, put some oil and lightly fry the eggplant pieces. Remove them from the pan. Using the same fry pan, add some oil. Then, add the cumin seeds and chopped garlic. Fry for 15 seconds, then add the onion and red pepper flakes. Fry for 3 minutes. Add the eggplants and stir it. Add the ketchup and mix it well.Add the vinegar and mix it. Cover the pan and keep on low heat for 8 minutes. Take off the lid, then add the salt and a little bit of water (if necessary). Cook for 4 minutes without the lid.Sprinkle the coriander over and turn off the heat.
mashed potatoas = potato (2 kg), peeled and diced, butter (345 g), 1 tablespoon salt, 1 teaspoon pepper, water (240 mL), milk (360 mL). lace potatoes, butter, salt, pepper, and water in a large slow cooker.Cover and cook on high for 4 hours.Remove lid and mash the potatoes with a masher or beaters, mixing in the milk.Stir in chives.Keep the slow cooker on “warm” setting while you serve them!
honey balsamic roasted brussels sprouts = brussels sprouts (680 g), 2 tablespoons oil, 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 2 teaspoons honey, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon pepper. Preheat oven to 425°F (220ºC).Slice the stem off of the Brussels sprouts and then halve them.On a prepared baking sheet, combine Brussels sprouts and remaining ingredients. Mix evenly.Bake for 15-20 minutes.
garlic parmesan roasted broccoli = 3 heads broccoli, 2 tablespoons oil, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, parmesan cheese (25 g), 2 teaspoons garlic powder,1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes,1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon pepper. Preheat oven to 400°F (200ºC).Separate the crowns and snip off any leaves of the broccoli.On a prepared baking sheet, combine broccoli and remaining ingredients. Mix evenly.Bake for 35-40 minutes.
butter permesan corn = 6 half ears corn, cleaned, husked2 tablespoons butter, softened, parmesan cheese (55 g). Preheat oven to 375ºF (190ºC).Coat each ear of corn in a thin layer of butter and roll in the parmesan cheese to coat.Place corn in a square baking dish and sprinkle with salt and pepper.Bake for 25 minutes.
